My Favourite Hobby


Why I love It!

Acting & Stand Up comedy is my Hobby,because it gives me an immense pleasure to keep my mind active and remove the negative mindset.Keeps my mind calm and happy all the time to stay positive and motivates throughout the day. It Boosts our personality and shows how brave we are in the society.It allows me to make people laugh and bring joy to their lives. The thrill of performing on stage, the challenge of crafting the perfect joke, and the satisfaction of hearing the audience's laughter are incredibly rewarding. It's a creative outlet that helps me connect with others and express myself in a unique and entertaining way.

  4. Before you perform an act in the stage,consider the check points ,whether the content goes violation of which you are delivering the act.
